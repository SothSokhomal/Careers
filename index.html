CAREER

A job is any legal activity that allows an individual to perform a service and in return earn credits she/he can use to buy things.

  • Aviation

flight attendant

They assist passengers in emergencies, keeping them calm and safe. They also serve beverages, snacks, and sometimes meals. This career involves both security and hospitality and offers an opportunity for individuals who want to provide safety and service to people while seeing the world.

Airline pilot

need to have the capacity to handle large aircraft and to make decisions with the best interests of all on board, and they need to be able to do so in adverse conditions. So, in addition to flight experience, a well-rounded education is important for prospective airline pilots.

  • Art

Artist

artist creates art such as paintings, sculptures, video, and film, sound works, drawings, printed works, installations, and other forms.

Architect

Architects design structures such as houses, apartment complexes, shopping centers, office buildings, and factories.

  • Business

Accountant

Accountants work with numbers, so they need to have strong mathematical skills. Accountants should be diligent and detail-oriented, as the job requires combing through volumes of financial data to search for problems and irregularities.
